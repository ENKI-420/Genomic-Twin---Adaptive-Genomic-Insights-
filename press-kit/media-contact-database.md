## Outreach Strategy by Publication Type

### Tier 1: Premier Publications (Nature, Science, MIT Tech Review, Forbes)
**Approach**: 
- Personalized pitches with exclusive angles
- Offer exclusive interviews with leadership
- Provide early access to research results
- Multiple follow-up touchpoints
**Timeline**: 2-4 week cultivation  
**Success Rate Target**: 25%

### Tier 2: Major Publications (TechCrunch, Wired, Bloomberg, IEEE)
**Approach**:
- Targeted pitches aligned with their coverage areas
- Provide comprehensive press materials
- Offer expert interviews and technical deep-dives
- 2-3 follow-up contacts
**Timeline**: 1-2 week cultivation  
**Success Rate Target**: 35%

### Tier 3: Industry Publications (VentureBeat, Ars Technica, Industry Journals)
**Approach**:
- Standard press release distribution
- Responsive to inquiries
- Provide technical details and access
- 1-2 follow-up contacts
**Timeline**: 1 week cultivation  
**Success Rate Target**: 50%
