**Frequency**: Bi-weekly

**Frequency**: Monthly  
**Distribution**: Website, sales materials, social media

**Purpose**: Thought leadership and vision

**Frequency**: Quarterly  
**Distribution**: YouTube, LinkedIn, media pitches

**Duration**: 20-45 minutes  
**Frequency**: As scheduled  
**Distribution**: Conference platforms, YouTube archive

## Production Specifications

### Video Quality Standards

**Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p) minimum, 4K for hero content  
**Frame Rate**: 30fps or 60fps  
**Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (YouTube, website), 9:16 (social shorts), 1:1 (Instagram)  
**File Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)  
**Audio**: AAC, 48kHz, stereo  

### Branding Guidelines

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: #2E7D32 (DNA Green)
- Secondary: #1565C0 (Cloud Blue)
- Accent: #7C4DFF (Quantum Purple)
- Background: #F5F5F5 (Light Gray)
- Text: #212121 (Dark Gray)

**Typography**:
- Headings: Inter Bold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Code: Source Code Pro

**Logo Usage**:
- Always use official logo files
- Minimum clear space: 1x logo height
- Never distort or recolor outside brand palette

### On-Screen Text Guidelines

**Lower Thirds (Name/Title)**:
- Font: Inter Semibold, 24pt
- Duration: 5-7 seconds minimum
- Position: Bottom third, left-aligned
- Animation: Slide in from left

**Captions/Subtitles**:
- Font: Inter Regular, 20pt
- Background: Semi-transparent black (70% opacity)
- Timing: Match audio precisely
- Position: Bottom center

**Highlighting/Callouts**:
- Use arrows or circles to highlight UI elements
- Animate in/out smoothly (0.3-0.5 seconds)
- Color: Quantum Purple (#7C4DFF)

## Video Distribution Strategy

### Platform-Specific Optimization

**YouTube**:
- Upload in highest quality available (4K preferred)
- Custom thumbnail (1280x720px)
- Detailed description with timestamps
- Relevant tags and categories
- Cards and end screens
- Playlist organization

**LinkedIn**:
- Upload natively (don't link to YouTube)
- Square or vertical format performs better
- Captions essential (autoplay is muted)
- Shorter cuts (1-3 minutes ideal)
- Tag relevant people and companies

**Twitter**:
- Native upload for better reach
- Subtitles required (autoplay muted)
- 2:20 maximum length
- Vertical or square format
- Attention-grabbing first 3 seconds

**Website**:
- Host on Wistia or Vimeo for analytics
- Responsive embed sizing
- Transcripts provided below video
- Related content links
- Lead capture integration
